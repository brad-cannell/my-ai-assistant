# Sora Image Prompt Adapt from EMS to HBPC-------------------------------------

Create a realistic, cinematic-style scene suitable for a PowerPoint presentation introducing a research project. The image should depict the adaptation of a health screening tool from pre-hospital emergency care to home-based primary care. Show a visual transition or contrast between two environments:

• On one side or earlier in the scene, emergency medical technicians (EMTs) or paramedics in uniform interacting with an older adult in their home, focusing on quick assessment and urgency.

• On the other side or later in the scene, a home-based primary care clinician (such as a nurse practitioner or physician) sitting with an older adult and caregiver in a calm, residential setting—warm lighting, personal touches like family photos or a cup of tea.

Convey the idea of adaptation, learning, and discovery—acknowledging that the team is exploring both what they know and what they don’t know about this new context.

No words, numbers, or text overlays. The mood should be professional, empathetic, and hopeful.

# Sora Prompt ----------------------------------------------------------------

Create a 10-second video clip illustrating adaptation strategies in healthcare research.

The scene shows two or three icon-style healthcare professionals collaborating around a table in a home-based primary care environment.
The people should look diverse, inclusive, and professional — stylized and simplified (like modern infographic characters), not cartoonish or hyper-realistic.

Show subtle motion such as clinicians discussing, pointing to a tablet, or reviewing a screening checklist together.
The setting should resemble a comfortable home interior used for clinical visits (e.g., light background furniture, soft neutral lighting).

The visual style should be flat, modern, and minimalist, with soft shading and gentle motion — similar to the aesthetic of health-care infographics or NIH research presentation visuals.

Prefer colors drawn from or harmonizing with this palette:
#ae6042 (warm clay), #4e738a (steel blue), #747578 (slate gray), #002856 (navy), #f2b826 (gold), #587e6a (sage green), #7D708f (lavender gray), #b0bfbc (light gray-green), #b6a999 (taupe).

Camera & Composition:
Medium-wide shot, smooth camera movement (slow pan or slight zoom).
Soft, natural lighting, neutral depth of field.

Do not include any text, numbers, logos, or captions.
Aspect ratio: 16 : 9 (landscape).
Duration: ~10 seconds.
Style tags (optional, if Sora supports them): flat design, minimalism, infographic aesthetic, professional healthcare setting, human connection, collaboration.
