# About me (delete if not needed)

I am an epidemiologist, gerontologist, data scientist, and associate professor at the University of Texas Health Science Center. I am passionate about researching healthy aging and late-life quality of life and applying that understanding to improving the health of older adults and communities. Specifically, I have published research focusing on preserving physical and cognitive function, living and aging with disability, and detecting and preventing elder mistreatment. I also have a passion for data science and digital epidemiology. I'm committed to the idea of learning about -- and positively impacting -- health outcomes using a data-driven approach. My expertise includes scientific writing – I have published more than 45 peer-reviewed journal articles, two textbook chapters, and one textbook; grant writing – I have secured more than $27 million in grant funding for research; project management – I have been the principal or co-investigator on multiple trials and observational studies in community and healthcare settings; teaching – I have taught 8 different courses at 3 different universities; public speaking – I have given numerous presentations and trainings; and data science – I am fluent in all major statistical programming languages.

# About Epi-Workbench

We are creating a web-based business called "Epi-Workbench." Epi-Workbench is an online platform offering interactive courses in epidemiology, data science, and artificial intelligence specifically designed for people working in public health and other health professions and, to a lesser extent, members of the general public who are interested in learning more about health research. With beginner to advanced-level courses, Epi-Workbench combines real-person instruction with hands-on coding exercises and integrated AI tools for a comprehensive learning experience. Learners can develop skills in statistical programming, applied statistical analyses, and scientific communication—all from the convenience of their browser, with no installation required.
