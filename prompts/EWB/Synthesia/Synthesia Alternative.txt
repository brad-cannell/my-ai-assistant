I have been using online software called Synthesia (https://www.synthesia.io/?r=0) to create training videos. My typical workflow is to:

1. Create a draft training video in PowerPoint.
2. Open Synthesia and create a training video based on the PowerPoint draft.
3. Use Synthesia's graphics, music, video library, and AI Voiceover tools to make the training videos look better.
4. Download an MP4 version of the final video, and download captions as a .srt file.
5. Upload the MP4 version of the video and captions to Vimeo.
6. Embed the Vimeo version of the training video on my website.

Synthesia and this process has worked pretty well, but it's also pretty expensive. I am currently paying $818.69 per year for Synthisia.

Before renewing my annual subscription for Synthesia in a couple of weeks, I'd like some help researching alternatives that are either cheaper than Synthesia, but performs just as well, or is better than Synthesia for roughly the same price.

Some critical needs to consider:
- Ability to create training videos from scratch. Either by uploading a PowerPoint presentation or by using a built-in video creation/editing tool.
- Create an AI Voiceover from a script.
- A music library.
- A image library or the ability to generate images using AI.
- A video library or the ability to generate videos using AI.
- The ability to create templates.
- The ability to download videos as MP4 files.
- The ability to download caption files for the video.

Please help me research Synthesia alternatives, and come up with a plan for moving forward.
