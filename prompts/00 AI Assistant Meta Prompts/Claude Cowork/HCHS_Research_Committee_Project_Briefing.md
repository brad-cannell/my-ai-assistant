The judge sign-up survey has 5 blocks:

1. **Introduction** — Purpose, event date/location, confidentiality note
2. **Judge Information** — Name, email, department/college
3. **Session Availability** — Checkbox for PS1 (10:00–11:00 AM) and/or PS2 (2:00–3:00 PM)
4. **Conflict of Interest** — Open text for disclosing any student relationships
5. **End of Survey** — Thank you + confirmation message
