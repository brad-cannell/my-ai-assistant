I am the chair of the Tenure and Promotion Committee for the Department of Applied Health Sciences, Harris College of Nursing and Health Sciences, Texas Christian University. 

We are a young department, and I have been charged with completing our first-ever round of annual reviews for faculty who are in the tenure stream, but not yet tenured.

Eventually, we will create our own departmental tenure and promotion criteria. For now, we will rely on the Harris College criteria (attached).

For each of the two faculty members being reviewed, I need to write an initial summary letter evaluating their progress toward tenure and promotion over the past academic year.  The letter should state for each area below whether the faculty member is making satisfactory or unsatisfactory progress. 

- Teaching
- Research
- Service
- Advising and/or related activities
- Professional development
- Conduct in accordance with the professional ethics
- Summary assessment

The tone of the letters is meant to be developmental, not punitive.
